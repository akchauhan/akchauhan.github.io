"***A BEAUTY WHICH IS LOVED YET***"

"How that spirit wandering,

Everyforenoon showing gestureness,

Pranced towards the arriving,

Nothing there but felt familiarness,

Perhaps came through whipping,

Made my heart pounding.


Chasing it with chivalric certainty,

Surrounding made me to feel solitary,

No creatures,No sign of life,No commotions at all there,

I felt myself in too much gloomyness,

Abruptly a sweet angel appeared with amber glow face,

Made me to feel my heart blitheness.


Still unaware of that spirit,

I hesitated a bit,

But her cuteness moved me far apart,

Leaving no questions in my heart.


Gazed at her face prolonged,

Totally oblivious to the world around,

It was nothing around except us,

Under the same sky and at the same sites.


By the by soulmate departed from my sight,

Showed me a gesture of love alright,

At the eleventh hour, I said,

A beauty whose beautiness reflects from her sanctity."


From the pen of A K CHAUHAN
